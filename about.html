FEATURES OF THE WEBSITE

This website allows the user to have a look at the furnitures in the store of Siku Furniture Home. A registered user is allowed to login and add items to their cart for purchase. During the checkout process, the user enters their location details and payment is done on delivery

ABOUT ME

My name is Busayo Akinsiku. I am a Plant Biotechnologist transitioning into tech, i am currently on my way to graduating from the ALX Software engineering course and this project was done in partial fulfillment for my certificate

The inspiration for this project came from my desire to help people beautify their homes to their taste, to help them source for lovely furnitures around the world and have it delivered at their doorstep at a very affordable price. Lots of people go to get furnitures but the tools and equipment used to make them are bad, but I want to recreate a trustworthy platform that allows you get lasting products and bridge the gap of buying synthetic products that won't last or buying products that are too costly and going through the stress of getting them. I want to provide what your money can get all from your comfort zone.
